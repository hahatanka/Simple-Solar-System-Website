OTHER PLANETS

There are more planets than stars in our galaxy. The inner, rocky planets that go around the Sun are Mercury, Venus, Earth, and Mars. NASA's newest rover — Perseverance — landed on Mars on Feb. 18, 2021. The outer planets are gas giants Jupiter and Saturn and ice giant Neptune.
